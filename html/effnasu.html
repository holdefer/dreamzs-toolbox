effnasu
Syntax 
E = effnasu(yObs,ySim)
Description

E = effnasu(yObs,ySim)

Function effnasu calculates the Nash-Sutcliffe efficiency E of 2 time series yObs and ySim. Variables yObs and ySim should have the same number of elements. If E is equal to 1 the time series are identical; for E below 0, the best predictor of ySim is in fact the mean of yObs.
